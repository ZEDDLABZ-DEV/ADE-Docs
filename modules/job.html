getJobsOfRequestedMill

  • getJobsOfRequestedMill(req: Request<any, any, any, { accommodation: string; area: string; city: string; date: string; industryType: string; isBanned: string; jobTitle: string; lat: string; long: string; medicalFacility: string; messFacility: string; millId: string; millName: string; page: string; peopleReq: string; salaryPerShift: string; size: string; state: string; transportation: string }, Record<string, any>>, res: Response<any, Record<string, any>>): Promise<any>
    • Defined in job.ts:367

    First of all it is checked whether the the request call is from CRM or App. For app, jobs are fetched from the redis server then these jobs are filtered on the basis of accommodation, medicalFacility, messFacility, transportation, jobTitle and industryType. Client can also filter the jobs on the basis of the latitude and longitude. Distance of every job is measured from thr given longitude and latitude, then jobs are sorted ascendingly and sent to the client. For CRM job can be filtered on other parameters as well such as millName, area, city, state, peopleReq, salaryPerShift and isBanned. Pagination is implemented for this API. So the client can also give page and size as parameter default page is 1 and size is 10.
